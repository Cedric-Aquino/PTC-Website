INFORMATION

Pateros Technological College (PTC) is a public higher education institution located in Pateros, Metro Manila, established in 1993 to provide affordable and accessible education to the local community. The college offers a range of programs in information technology, business, and technical-vocational fields, aimed at equipping students with practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge. Committed to academic excellence and community development, PTC continues to prepare students for productive careers and meaningful contributions to society.

VISION
