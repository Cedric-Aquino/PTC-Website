OUR SERVICES

SERVICES OFFERED

Pateros Technological College (PTC) offers a range of educational services designed to equip students with practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge. The college provides technical-vocational training, associate and bachelor’s degree programs in fields such as information technology, education, hospitality management, and office administration. It also supports students through hands-on learning, computer-based instruction, and opportunities for internships and off-campus training, helping prepare graduates for employment and career advancement.

ORGANIZATIONS

At Pateros Technological College (PTC), student organizations such as JPCS, JPASAPS, IMAGE, THE PLUMAGE, PMO, SFHRE, GIFT’D, CHAMBERS, GROOVERS, and the Drum & Lyre Corps play a vital role in enriching campus life. These organizations provide students with opportunities to develop leadership skills, showcase their talents, and participate in academic, cultural, and extracurricular activities. Through active involvement, they help build a strong sense of community and prepare students for future personal and professional success.

SCHOLARSHIPS

At Pateros Technological College (PTC), students are supported through various financial assistance programs such as the Barangay Scholar program, Tulong Dunong Program (TDP), and Tertiary Education Subsidy (TES). These initiatives help ease the financial burden of education by providing allowances and support to deserving students, enabling them to focus on their studies and successfully complete their academic journey.

Tertiary Education Subsidy (TES)

Financial Assistance from CHED with collaboration with DSWD and belong to 4P's.

TES is a grant-in-aid program under RA 10931 or Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act given to qualified students enrolled in CHED-recognized public and private higher education institutions to support at least the partial cost of tertiary education, inclusive of education-related expenses. It also provides access to quality tertiary education in city/town without state or local universities and colleges (SUCs/LUCs).
